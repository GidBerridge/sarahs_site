Services

Some of these are terms are derogatory and  I would prefer not to use them. I've included them here as they are popular and may lead people to seek help

  • Vulvodynia (persistent pain / pain on contact with the vulva)

  • Erectile dysfunctions including rapid / premature and delayed / retarded ejaculation
  • Orgasm problems (including anorgasmia)
  • Sexual phobias, aversion or fear of sex 
  • Sexual compulsive behaviour and sex addiction
  • Communication difficulties and arguments
  • Domestic abuse
  • Affairs
  • Jealousy
  • Sex with a disability 
  • Problems arising from Tourette's, tics and twitches
  • Sex with a mental disability
  • Sex with learning difficulties
  • Anxiety around forming relationships or commitment issues
  • Sexual abuse, incest or rape
  • Managing a relationship that is failing 
  • Sexual orientation concerns
  • Fears over “troubling” fantasies 
  • Differing libidos / preferences within a relationship 
  • Curiosity over asexuality
  • Exploring open relationships
  • Worries concerning kink, BDSM and fetish fantasies / behaviour
  • Gender dysphoria
  • Problems as a result of sex working
  • ​Chem sex
  • Use of illegal porn, indecent exposure and other offending behaviour

If you are experiencing a sex or relationship problem that is not on this list, do feel free to call or email as I may be able to help. If I cannot, I will try and direct you to someone who can.

Please be aware: This is not a sex surrogacy service or a sex coaching service. There is NO sexual contact.
