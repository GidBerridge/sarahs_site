Sarah Berry: Sex & Relationship Therapist

Arranging a Session

Sessions are usually £100 for individuals and £150 for couples. I do offer concessions for students, low waged, pensioners and those on benefits.
I only work online over Zoom, FaceTime or Skype or over the phone.

The next step:

  • If you would like to book a session, send me a text, write me an email or leave me a voicemail.
  • If you want me to phone you, and there is a particular time you don't want me to call, please state this in the message.
  • We then arrange an sessions on the phone or over email.
  • I usually see clients on a weekly basis. If this isn't possible, I do have a few slots available for fortnightly or ad hoc sessions.
  • Feel free to arrange an initial session. After this, you can let me know if you would like to commit to therapy. Do note that you can leave any time.
