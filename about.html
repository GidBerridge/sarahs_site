Sarah Berry: Sex & Relationship Therapist

About Me

Growing up I had a lot sadness and shame surrounding having the psychosexual issue vaginismus which leads to pain during intercourse. So I looked for ways to compensate.

I became the editor of the sadly now defunct fetish magazine Forum. Following this, I set up a women's support group at Sh! Women's Store called Fannying Around. While the name may seem irreverent, the group attracted many people who would not usually seek out a therapist but needed a safe space where they could discuss feeling and thoughts surrounding their bodies and sex lives.

While I struggled to find help from therapists, at work I learnt a huge amount about myself and other people and finally trained to be the therapist I wished I had.

I now specialise in sexual pain and arousal issues as well as difficulties surrounding kink and fetish, gender identity and opening up relationships. I am also qualified and experienced at working with sexual compulsivity and sex offending.

It's a huge honour to help other people through their issues to live the fulfilling life that they deserve.

Training and Memberships

  • London Diploma in sexual and relationship therapy, awarded by COSRT
  • Advanced Couples Diploma, awarded by COSRT
  • Working with Sex Offenders - Certificate, awarded by STOPSO
  • Certificate in sexual compulsivity awarded by ATSAC

I am an accredited member of COSRT (College of Sex & Relationship Therapists) and also a member of ATSAC ( Association for the Treatment of Sexual Addiction and Compulsivity), sex offender rehabilitation charity StopSo and Pink Therapy. I'm fully insured and work under the COSRT ethical guidelines - including those related to client confidentiality.
